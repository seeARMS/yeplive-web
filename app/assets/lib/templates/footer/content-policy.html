Yeplive™ (“Yeplive”) requires that all its users comply with the following Content Policy intended to help to ensure our users stay out of trouble and to keep Yeplive a safe environment for all. Please adhere to this policy and do not abuse our Yeplive Services.

You hereby agree, represent and warrant that you will not post:

  • Content which impersonates another person and/or content which is meant to deceive or mislead.
    • Content which impersonates another person or content which is meant to mislead or deceive is not allowed. Fan accounts, or parody accounts should be clearly labelled as such. For example, in the “about you” section make mention of the fact that it is a parody account or use a descriptive username, e.g., “@NotReallyBeyonce”.
  • Content which is pornographic or which is overtly sexual.
  • Content which is explicitly graphic.
    • Explicitly graphic content is not allowed. Examples of such content may include but are not limited to: abuse of any kind, bodily injury or harm, cruelty towards animals, content which includes direct or indirect threats to others and content which is meant to incite violence or criminal behaviour of any kind.
  • Content or media which is illegal, or which may incite violence or dangerous/illegal activity.
  • Content which is meant to disrespect, harass or abuse other users.
  • Content which violates the rights of a third party, including but not limited to copyright, trademark, privacy and/or publicity.
  • Content which includes the private information of other people.
    • Yeplive does not allow content which includes the private information of other people. Such information may include but is not limited to social insurance/social security numbers or any type of national identity number, credit card information, non-public phone numbers or email addresses and private addresses.

    Yeplive reserves the right to allow certain sensitive information, where it makes sense considering local privacy laws, the nature and context of the information and/or other facts which may assist in determining whether the information is to be considered Private Information within this policies definition.

  • Content which would be considered Spam. Do not Spam.
    • Yeplive does not allow spamming of any kind, abuse of this policy will result in the permanent suspension of the users account without notice or warning.
    • The following is a non-exhaustive list of examples of types of spam:
      • The creation of serial accounts intended for abusive or disruptive purposes.
      • Consistent following and unfollowing of other users
      • Participating in or encouraging the purchase or sale of Yeplive accounts in breach of our Terms of Use.
      • Malware / Phishing – interfere or damage the Yeplive Service or the use and/or enjoyment of other users by spreading, dispersing or uploading any malicious code including but not limited to viruses, malware, adware, spyware, worm or Trojan.
      • Broadcast Spam
      • Register more than five (5) accounts.

Yeplive reserves the right to allow certain information where it is deemed by us to be relevant or newsworthy, or of social importance despite it being potentially sensitive in nature. For more information please feel free to contact us at: legal@yeplive.com
